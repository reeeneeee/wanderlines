How it works

Wanderlines
Tap a countryIts name pops up and the color cycles: Visited → Lived → Wish List → clear.
Press & holdOpens details to change the list, set the year, or add notes.
SearchFind a country, state/province, National Park, UNESCO site or airport and jump to it.
KeyBottom-left chip: set the year for new taps and toggle overlays: Parks, UNESCO, My Journeys.
JourneysLog a trip: its stops, who came, and a journal with photos, then replay its route on the map.
RegionsScratch off states & provinces (incl. U.S. territories) inside many countries.
TimelineSee when you went where, by year. Pinch to add cities & landmarks.
Passport & vibesAccount holds your stamps, flags & badges, 6 vibes, miles/km, and native names.
MenuSwitch screens. Tap the logo any time to come home.
